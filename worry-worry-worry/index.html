This Worry Tree app is based on a mindfulness technique originally developed by Butler and Hope in their Managing Your Mind book.

The purpose is to help you identify, address, and act on worries or concerns.

If you are currently feeling suicidal, please reach out to a friend or resource. The National Suicide Prevention Hotline can help.
